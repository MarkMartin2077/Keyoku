# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 16, 2026**

## Overview

Keyoku ("the App") is a flashcard study app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information provided by those services (such as your name and email address). You may also use the App anonymously without providing any personal information.

### Usage Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics and Mixpanel to collect anonymized usage data, such as:

- Which screens you visit
- Which features you use (e.g., creating decks, studying flashcards)
- App performance and crash data
- Device type and operating system version

This data helps us understand how the App is used and improve the experience. Analytics data is not linked to your identity in a way that would allow us to identify you personally.

### Content You Create

Flashcard decks and their content are stored in Firebase Firestore and on your device. This data is associated with your account so it can sync across your devices.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the App
- To sync your flashcard data across devices
- To understand usage patterns and improve the App
- To send study reminders (only if you enable notifications)

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

- **Firebase** (Google) — Authentication, data storage, analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications
- **Mixpanel** — Usage analytics
- **RevenueCat** — In-app purchase management
- **Apple Sign In / Google Sign In** — Authentication

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your personal data with any other third parties.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore with security rules that restrict access to your own data. Flashcard content is also cached locally on your device for offline access.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings within the App. When you delete your account:

- Your user profile is permanently deleted
- All your flashcard decks and content are permanently deleted
- Your analytics profile is removed

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the App Store listing.
