FAQ

  • How do I create a deck? Tap the + button on the Home screen, give your deck a name, and paste or type your source text.
  • Can I use Keyoku offline? Yes. Your decks are saved locally and sync when you're back online.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Go to Profile > Settings > Restore Purchases.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.
